Student Bio

Xingxian Shou

Xingxian Shou was born in Zheiang Province in China and attended university in Beijing. He came to United States in 2006 and studied physics with Dr. Brown. He completed his graduate study in MRI physics and got his PhD in 2011. He has been working as an MRI imaging scientist. He focused on developing advanced MRI clinical applications for their 1.5 T system, including various projects like DTI and Cardiac.
